Welcome to the Cecil B. Moore Vision Zero project's public engagement map!

The Delaware Valley Regional Planning Commission (DVRPC) and the City of Philadelphia's Office of Transportation, Infrastructure, and Sustainability (oTIS) are studying Cecil B. Moore Avenue between 10th Street and Willington Street, with the goal of improving safety for all road users. This map was created to help you share your thoughts about the study area with the DVRPC / oTIS project team.

  • Click the yellow button to add a comment about a specific place
  • Click the blue button to tell us how you use Cecil B. Moore Avenue today, and what your vision is for its future
  • To learn more, please visit the project's website
